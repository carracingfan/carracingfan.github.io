Vegetable Dip

    Ingredients:


  • 1/2 Cup Buttermilk
  • 4 ounces fat free cream cheese
  • 3 green onions
  • 1/4 cup green pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 Tbls. snipped fresh dill or 1 tbls. dill weed
  • 4 1/2 teaspoons horse radish sauce
  • 8 teaspoons garlic powder
  • Assorted fresh Vegetables

Directions:


  1. In a small bowl, beat buttermilk and cream cheese until well blended
  2. Add all other ingredients
  3. Stir well, chill before serving.
  4. Serve with vegetables.
