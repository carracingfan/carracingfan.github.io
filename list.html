    Sausage Balls

    This recipe is sure to be a hit at your next family gathering. Described as a perfect blend of sausage, cheese and biscuit mix. They will have even the finickiest finger food finatics saying, "ooh they are so doggone delicious!"

    Ingredients:


  • 16 oz. breakfast sausage
  • 16 oz. sharp cheddar Cheese
  • 3 cups Bisquick biscuit mix

Directions:


  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Mix sausage and cheese together.
  3. Add Bisquick to mixture and mix well.
  4. Roll mixture into balls similar in size to a walnut.
  5. Place balls on ungreased baking sheet, leaving about 1 inch space between each, and bake for 20 minutes.

    6. -- Makes about 40 sausage balls --

Tips for best results:

  • Grate your own cheese.
    • Seems to mix better if cheese is freshly grated.
    • Cheese is moister making for a better bind.
  • Use small measuring cup when rolling balls.
    • This helps keep the size of balls uniform so they will bake evenly.
  • Turn oven to broil during last 2 minutes of baking time.
    • This will brown the sausage balls.


    Remarks:
    This is my wife's go to party appetizer, especially during the Christmas season. I'll admit, she has her hands full keeping me from eating most of them before they make it to the party!
