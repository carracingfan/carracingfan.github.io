Dad's Memory Machine

Poppy Sparks along with Thenia Waddel, his daughter, talk to Ernest

My This recording was made at Aunt Thenia's house on Dry Fork in the mid 60's. They discussed a fishing trip that Poppy and my grandfather, John Sparks, took sometime between 1955 & 1958. They had journeyed to Chilhowie and fished with my Dad, Ernest, and my grandfather, Scott Heath.
